      Delivery Locations

      We deliver flowers to most locations within the country. Unfortunately, we do not ship internationally at this time. If you have any questions regarding our delivery area, please contact our customer service team before placing your order.


      Delivery Times

      Everyday between 10AM to 8PM (local time).


      Shipping Charges
      • Inside Divisional City: $25
      • Outside Divisional City: $35

      Packaging and Handling

      Our flowers are carefully packaged to ensure they arrive in perfect condition. Each bouquet is hand-arranged and wrapped in eco-friendly materials. We include a complimentary message card with every order.


      Order Tracking

      Once your order has been dispatched, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your delivery in real-time.


      Delivery Issues

      If no one is available to accept the delivery, our courier will leave the flowers in a safe place or with a neighbor, and a notification will be left at the delivery address. If the flowers cannot be delivered, we will attempt to contact you to arrange an alternative delivery time.


      Weather Conditions

      In extreme weather conditions, delivery may be delayed for the safety of our delivery personnel. We will notify you of any delays and reschedule the delivery as soon as possible.


      Returns and Refunds

      Due to the perishable nature of our products, we cannot accept returns. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service team within 24 hours of delivery. We will do our best to resolve any issues and may offer a replacement or refund at our discretion.


      Customer Service

      If you have any questions or concerns regarding your order or our shipping policy, please contact our customer service team at rosarium@gmail.com or call us at 0123456789. Our team is available everyday, 10 AM to 8 PM (local time).
